Magdalena Naja
Born on September 24, 1991, in Lublin, the city where the eternal war for the rule of the artists and dreadlords. From an early age, she showed interest in computer graphics, creating her first, extremely sublime and realistic work in the advanced program for the original artist - Painta on Windows 95. After the great IV. A. Mickiewicz in Warsaw went on an artistic journey to graphic studios for the notorious Kazimierz Dolny. The bachelor graduated with honors from the dean of the UMCS Art Faculty for his diploma - a set of covers for the novel by Charles Bukowski made in workshop techniques (serigraphy and linocut). With a college diploma (College of Fine Arts in Kazimierz Dolny), which was the last year, like a graduate of Hogwarts she began to look for work. After half a year of wandering and small jobs for Intenson, she got into a coveted internship, which then turned into a coveted job at an advertising agency. After more than a year and a half of wonderful and successful cooperation with ASAP & ASAP Communication, she decided to train herself.
